Our BS Programs

BS Clinical Sciences

The BS Clinical Sciences program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of medical practices, laboratory science, and patient care. This program is ideal for students seeking to enter the healthcare field with a solid foundation in clinical knowledge.
