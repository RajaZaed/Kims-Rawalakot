Our History

Founded in 2017, KIMS started as a small institution with the aim of offering specialized medical training in the region. Over the years, we have expanded our programs and campuses, incorporating state-of-the-art technology and a modern curriculum. Our faculty members have extensive experience in healthcare and academia, and they bring a wealth of knowledge to our students. Today, KIMS is recognized as one of the top institutions for medical education in the region, offering both undergraduate and graduate programs that cater to the diverse needs of aspiring healthcare professionals.

What Sets Us Apart

What makes KIMS unique is our unwavering commitment to student success. We provide a range of support services, from career counseling to mentoring and extracurricular activities, ensuring that every student is supported throughout their academic journey. Our students have the opportunity to gain practical experience through internships and clinical rotations, which prepare them for the challenges of real-world healthcare environments.

Our Achievements

Throughout the years, KIMS has achieved numerous milestones, including the successful graduation of over 5,000 healthcare professionals who are now working in various medical fields both locally and internationally. Our graduates have earned a reputation for their competency, professionalism, and dedication to improving healthcare systems worldwide. Additionally, KIMS has contributed to various research initiatives aimed at improving healthcare delivery in the region.
